Uniplaces Differences

This is what makes us different.

No bureaucracies

Forget all about selection processes and in-person viewings. Save your time by discovering unique homes and booking them online, from anywhere in the world.

Tenants' protection

After paying your first month's rent to Uniplaces, we wait until 24 hours after you move-in to transfer it to the landlord. This allows you to confirm that everything is ok with your new home.

Wide selection

We offer many different kinds of properties, our listings include authentic photos, videos of verified places, you can search by price, type of property, house features and rules, this way you don’t need to visit them.

Service fee

Our service fee covers a wide range of services including no need for guarantor, a multilingual support team, a 24 hours policy move-in, a place accuracy check, and an official prepaid accommodation certificate.

Certificate of accommodation

In case you need an official document, we provide a prepaid accommodation certificate that you can use on your Visa application, which proves that you have booked a place to stay.

Verified places

We’re here to make your life easier, so all places go through our team who verifies all the information provided to our platform, verifying the veracity of the information for a total peace of mind.
